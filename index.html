About Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s.

How to Use This Timer:

  1. Choose a task to focus on
  2. Start the 25-minute timer (work session)
  3. Work focused until the timer rings
  4. Take a 5-minute break
  5. After 4 work sessions, take a 30-minute break
  6. Change tasks after each Pomodoro to maintain productivity

Key Productivity Tips:

  • Switch tasks every 25 minutes to maintain fresh focus
  • Use work notes to track different tasks
  • During breaks, completely disengage from work
  • Adjust timer lengths based on task complexity
  • Use the 5-minute breaks to stretch and refresh
